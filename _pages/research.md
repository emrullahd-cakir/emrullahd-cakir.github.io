My research interests sit at the intersection of astrophysics, observation, and scientific computing. I am especially interested in how detector response, source morphology, and statistical modeling affect the claims we make from data.

* **High-energy astronomy notes and analysis workflows.** A developing collection of short explanations, scripts, and worked examples related to X-ray data analysis.

* **Reproducible scientific computing.** Experiments with analysis structure, calibration steps, and readable code for physics workflows.

## Selected topics

* Forward folding and detector response
* Point-spread functions and source localization
* Source flux, luminosity, and physical interpretation
