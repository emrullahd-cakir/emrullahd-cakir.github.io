Research

Questions before conclusions.

I am interested in the methods that connect X-ray observations to robust physical interpretation.

Focus areas

  • Source characterization

    Understanding the position, brightness, spectrum, and time behavior of X-ray sources, and how those measurements constrain their nature.

  • Response and calibration

    Studying how instrumental response, point-spread functions, and calibration choices enter an analysis before a physical model is evaluated.

  • Statistical modeling

    Using forward-folded models and careful uncertainty treatment to compare theory with counts recorded by a detector.

Research practice

I value transparent analysis: documented assumptions, readable code, explicit units, and results that can be reproduced from the original data products.

My current technical work emphasizes Python-based scientific computing and structured workflows for astronomical catalogs and observations.
