About

My work and study sit at the intersection of observation, modeling, and scientific computing. I am especially interested in how calibration, detector response, and analysis choices shape what we can reliably learn from astronomical data.

This website collects developing research interests, technical notes, and selected academic work.

Current focus

  • High-energy astronomy

    X-ray sources, source characterization, and the physical interpretation of energetic astrophysical systems.

  • Detector response

    Calibration, forward folding, point-spread functions, and the observational systematics behind measured quantities.

  • Scientific computing

    Readable, reproducible analysis workflows for working with research data and testing physical models.
