About

I am an astrophysics student interested in high-energy astronomy, data analysis, and scientific computing. I am especially interested in how calibration, detector response, and modeling choices shape the physical claims we make from observations.

This website collects developing research interests, technical notes, and selected academic work.

Current interests

  • X-ray astronomy and source characterization. Understanding the position, brightness, spectrum, and time behavior of energetic sources.
  • Detector response and observational systematics. Calibration, forward folding, and the instrumental effects behind measured quantities.
  • Reproducible scientific computing. Readable analysis workflows for working with research data and testing physical models.
